3. User Stories
These stories focus on functionalities that provide value without the need for account configuration.
1. Parameter Selection: As a user, I want to choose a topic (Spring or Angular) and the number of questions (10, 20, 30) to tailor the quiz to my time and knowledge.
    ◦ Acceptance Criteria (AC): Two technology tiles available; dropdown or buttons for the number of questions; no login requirement.
2. Interaction with a Question: As a user, I want to select answers in an interactive form to simulate a knowledge test.
    ◦ AC: Displaying one question at a time; 4 options to choose from; a "Next" button.
3. Immediate Result: As a user, I want to see my percentage score after finishing the test to assess my level of advancement.
    ◦ AC: Display of the total points and percentage of correct answers immediately after the last question.
Data Management: Quiz data (questions) should now be loaded directly from a database or configuration file by the backend without permission verification.
